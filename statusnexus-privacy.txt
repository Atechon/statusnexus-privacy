# Privacy Policy for StatusNexus

**Last updated: June 2026**

## What StatusNexus Does
StatusNexus is a WhatsApp and Telegram status viewer and downloader for Android.
It reads status media that you have already viewed in WhatsApp or Telegram,
and saves copies to your device gallery upon your request.

## Data We Collect
StatusNexus does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data to
any server. All media files remain on your device only.

We use **Google AdMob** to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:
- Device advertising ID
- Approximate location (country/region)
- App usage information

This data is collected by Google, not by us. See Google's privacy policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Permissions Used
- **Storage / Media**: To read WhatsApp/Telegram statuses and save
  downloaded files to your gallery.
- **Biometric**: To protect the Vault feature (optional, device-only).
- **Notifications**: To notify you when downloads complete (optional).
- **Internet**: Required to load advertisements.

## Third-Party Services
- Google AdMob (advertising) — https://policies.google.com/privacy
- Google Play Services

## Data Retention
No user data is retained by StatusNexus. Media files saved to your device
can be deleted by you at any time through the app or your device gallery.

## Children's Privacy
StatusNexus is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly
collect data from children.
